# EaglEs Property — Implementation Roadmap

> MVP → Enterprise → Intelligence → Global Scale

---

## Phase 1: Foundation & Core Modules (Months 1–3)
**Goal**: Launch MVP for Developer-Sales workflow.

- **Infrastructure**: CI/CD pipelines, Multi-tenant Security Rules, Auth flows.
- **Module 1 (Developer Mgmt)**: Profile, employees, branding.
- **Module 2 (Project Mgmt)**: Project setup, tower/floor definitions.
- **Module 4 (Inventory)**: Unit matrix, availability tracking.
- **Module 5 (Sales CRM)**: Lead capture, pipeline, reservations.
- **Module 13 (Authentication)**: Basic login & tenant switching.

---

## Phase 2: Transactional & Construction (Months 4–6)
**Goal**: Handle payments, contracts, and site progress.

- **Module 3 (Construction)**: Schedule, daily reports, photo logs.
- **Module 7 (Customer Portal)**: Mobile-optimized payment portal, my documents.
- **Module 10 (Finance)**: Invoices, payment gateway integration (Stripe, Telebirr).
- **Module 11 (Documents)**: DMS, PDF generation for contracts.
- **Module 6 (Marketplace)**: Public listing site, search, and map integration.

---

## Phase 3: Operations & Intelligence (Months 7–9)
**Goal**: Full property lifecycle and AI-driven insights.

- **Module 8 (Rental Mgmt)**: Lease management, tenant portal.
- **Module 9 (Facilities Mgmt)**: Work orders, asset tracking.
- **Module 12 (AI Assistant)**: Contract OCR, AI lead scoring, construction delay prediction.
- **Module 14 (Analytics)**: KPI dashboards, BigQuery integration.
- **Offline-First Enhancement**: Advanced sync strategies and media queues.

---

## Phase 4: Enterprise Scale & Ecosystem (Months 10+)
**Goal**: Open platform for consultants and ecosystem partners.

- **External Portals**: Dedicated access for Contractors, Banks, and Architects.
- **Advanced 3D**: Digital Twin integration, Matterport deeper mapping.
- **Global Expansion**: Multi-currency, localized tax engines, regional deployments.
- **API Platform**: Public API for third-party integrations.

---

## Key Milestones

1.  **Alpha (End of Month 2)**: Core inventory and lead management live for internal testing.
2.  **Beta (End of Month 5)**: First property sale completed end-to-end on the platform.
3.  **V1.0 (End of Month 7)**: Public Marketplace and Construction monitoring launched.
4.  **V2.0 (End of Month 12)**: AI-first features fully integrated into all modules.
